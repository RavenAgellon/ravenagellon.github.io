About Me

School

It was a unique experience for me during my time at Singapore Polytechnic. I was exposed to a class that came from diverse backgrounds. Despite the challenges that affected my third year due to the Circuit Breaker in 2020, I had to discipline myself to conduct my own research and still be able to submit my work on time.
